13. 5! - 4! ========== knp dihari yg cerah gini, rumah w sepi :v


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]5! - 4! \\ (5 \times 4 \times 3 \times 2) - (4 \times 3 \times 2) \\ 120 - 24 \\ = 96[/tex]

1.) 5! - 4!

= ( 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 ) - ( 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 )

= ( 20 × 3 × 2 × 1 ) - ( 12 × 2 × 1 )

= ( 60 × 2 × 1 ) - ( 24 × 1 )

= ( 120 × 1 ) - 24

= 120 - 24

= 96

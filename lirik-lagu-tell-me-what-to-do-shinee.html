The word 'too' is a determiner that refers to an excessive degree or something that is more than enough. Some examples of the usage of 'too' are:

a. He is too stubborn to admit his mistakes.

a. I'm not brave enough for politics.

40. Apa passive voice dari "They didn't tell us what to do"


Jawaban:

Passive voice dari "They didn't tell us what to do" adalah

We weren't told what to do by them.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas berupa kalimat negatif passive tense.

Rumus passive past tense adalah object + was/were + verb 3 + by subject.

Untuk mengubahnya menjadi passive voice, diubah terlebih menjadi active voice:

Active: They told us what to do. (they - subject, told - predicate, us - object, what to do - complement)

Passive: We were told what to do by them.

Negative passive: we weren't told what to do by them.

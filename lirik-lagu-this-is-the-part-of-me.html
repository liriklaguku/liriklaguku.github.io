Dalam bahasa Inggris, setiap kata dikelompokkan ke dalam 8 (delapan) macam part of speech sebagai berikut:

Noun (Kata Benda)

Part of speech ini berfungsi untuk menamai orang, tempat, benda, atau ide. Umumnya, noun didahului oleh partikel a, an,dan the. Noun juga dapat berbentuk singular atau plural dan konkrit atau abstrak. Dalam suatu kalimat, noun dapat berfungsi sebagai subjek, objek langsung, objek tidak langsung, pelengkap subjek, atau objek dari suatu preposisi.

Contoh:

The teacher brought some foods for the class because all students in the class passes the final exam.

Pronoun (Kata Ganti Benda)

Part of speech ini digunakan sebagai kata ganti noun dalam suatu kalimat. Pronoun umumnya berfungsi agar tidak terjadi pengulangan kata yang dapat menghasilkan kalimat yang panjang dan tidak lazim.

Kalimat tanpa Pronoun Kalimat dengan Pronoun

Beyonce held a concert last week in Jakarta. Although Beyonce felt exhausted due to long flight, Beyonce was still very excited to visit Jakarta for the first time. Nevertheless, Beyonce promised to visit Jakarta again when Beyonce had the opportunity. Beyonce held a concert last week in Jakarta. Although she felt exhausted due to long flight, she was still very excited to visit Jakarta for the first time. Nevertheless, she promised to visit Jakarta again when she had the opportunity.

Dari contoh di atas, noun adalah Beyonce dan pronoun yang menggantikannya adalah she. Noun yang digantikan oleh suatu pronoun disebut antecedent.

Terdapat 6 (enam) jenis pronoun:

Personal pronoun

Sebagai pengganti orang, tempat, atau benda secara spesifik.

I, you, we, they, he, she, it,

Me, you, us, them, him, her, it.

The parts of the tree have their respective roles and functions, which are as follows:

1.Stalk The stalk itself is a branch of the stem that functions to support the flower. In a perfect flower there is a stigma (stigma), stamen (filament which is part of the stamens), and flower stalk (pedicel).

2. Flower Petals (Calyx) Flower petals are the flower jewelry that is on the outside, which is usually often flashy or colorful. The flower petals protect the flower while it is still a bud. The flower petals are also called as being composed of several petals (spala).

3. Flower Crown (Corolla) The flower crown is called a large and usually colorful flower ornament (periantheum), which is composed of several petals (petala). the flower crown functions to attract insects, so that they suck honey to help pollinate.

bahasa Indonesia = bagian ini menopang mahkota pohon.

1. Tangkai Tangkai sendiri merupakan cabang dari batang yang berfungsi untuk menopang bunga. Pada bunga sempurna terdapat kepala putik (stigma), benang sari (benang sari yang merupakan bagian dari benang sari), dan tangkai bunga (pedicel).

3. Mahkota Bunga (Corolla) Mahkota bunga disebut hiasan bunga yang besar dan biasanya berwarna-warni (periantheum), mahkota bunga berfungsi menarik serangga, sehingga menghisap madu untuk membantu penyerbukan.

29. 1. what is the name of this text?2. what part of the generic structure?


1. Narative text
-> Narative text adalah teks yang menceritakan tentang khayalan. seperti: putri duyung, cinderella, rabbit and crocodile. Termasuk teks fabel yang dimana tokoh nya adalah para binatang dan bertingkah laku seperti manusia (bisa bicara)

2. Orientation, complication, resolution
-> Orientation: berisi pengenalan/pembuka suatu cerita atau berisi tentang penyebab masalah. (Di soal, ada di paragraf 1)
Complication: berisi masalah / konflik yang terjadi dalam suatu cerita. (Ada di paragraf 2 dan 3)
Resolution: berisi tentang selesainya masalah di cerita tersebut. (ada di paragraf 4)-Narrative text is a story with complication or problematic events and it tries to find the resolutions to solve the problems. An important part of narrative text is the narrative mode, the set of methods used to communicate the narrative through a process narration.


-Orientation : It is about the opening paragraph where the characters of the story are introduced.
# Complication : Where the problems in the story developed.
# Resolution : Where the problems in the story is solved.
# Coda / reorientation (optional) – lesson from the story

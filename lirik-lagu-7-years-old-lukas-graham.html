22. my grandmother is 56 years old. my mom is 31 years old. i am 7 years old. in how many years is the sum of our ages 100?​


Jawab:

The sum of their ages = 100 will be in 2 years.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Sum of their ages = (Gran's age + x) + (Mom's age + x) + (My age + x)

100 = 56 + x + 31 + x + 7 + x

100 = 94 + 3x

100 - 94 = 3x

6 = 3x

x = 6 / 3
